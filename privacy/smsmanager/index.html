Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026 | Erhan Güngör | info@applicalgap.com

1. Data Collection and Infrastructure

To provide synchronization and account-based management, the following configuration data is stored in our secure server-side PostgreSQL database: Blocked sender names, manually created contact names and phone numbers, and user-defined forwarding rules. This data is linked to your account via Clerk.

SMS Content Privacy: The actual body and content of your SMS messages are NEVER transmitted to or stored on our servers. All filtering and forwarding logic is executed strictly on your local device.

2. Authentication & Third Parties

Account authentication is managed by Clerk. Your sign-in credentials (email/phone) are governed by Clerk’s Privacy Policy (clerk.com/privacy). Subscription entitlements are managed by RevenueCat, while all financial transactions are handled exclusively by Apple or Google.

3. Data Retention & Security

We maintain server-side data using encrypted connections (SSL/TLS) and strict access controls. Data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request permanent deletion of your account and associated data by contacting info@applicalgap.com.
