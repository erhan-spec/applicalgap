Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026 | Developer: Erhan Güngör

1. Introduction

SMS Manager is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy covers compliance with the UK GDPR, Australian Privacy Act 1988, and US State laws (CCPA/CPRA).

Zero-Cloud Policy: We do not store, upload, or sell your SMS data. All processing occurs locally on your device.

2. Data Collection and Use

We process the following data locally to provide core functionality:

  • Sender IDs/Numbers (for filtering).
  • SMS Body Content (only processed by local logic for forwarding/blocking).
  • Forwarding Target Numbers.

Note for California (US) Residents: We do not "Sell" or "Share" your personal information as defined by the CCPA.

3. International Compliance

UK & EU (GDPR): The legal basis for processing is the performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)). You have the right to access and delete your local logs.

Australia: We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles. Data is handled strictly within the bounds of your mobile device.
