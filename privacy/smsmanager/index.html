Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026 | Erhan Güngör | info@applicalgap.com

1. Introduction

SMS Manager is committed to transparency. This policy explains how we collect, store, and process your data in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and the Australian Privacy Act.

2. Data Collection and Usage

To operate the service and provide cloud-based synchronization across your devices, the following data is stored in our secure server-side PostgreSQL database and linked to your account:

  • Blocked sender names and contact names.
  • Contact phone numbers managed within the app.
  • User-defined SMS forwarding rules.
Important: The actual body/content of your SMS messages is NEVER transmitted to or stored on our servers. Processing of message content for filtering and forwarding occurs entirely on your device.

3. Account and Authentication

Users must create an account to access the app's features. We use Clerk as our third-party authentication provider. When you sign in via email or phone, your credentials are processed securely by Clerk. You can review their privacy practices at clerk.com/privacy.

4. Device Permissions

  • READ_SMS / RECEIVE_SMS: To identify and filter incoming messages locally based on your rules.
  • SEND_SMS: To facilitate the forwarding feature via your cellular network.

Note: The app does NOT read from your device's native phone contact book. All contacts managed in SMS Manager are created manually by the user.

5. Data Security

Your configuration data is stored in a server-side database protected by industry-standard security measures. This includes encrypted connections (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and strict server-side access controls for data at rest. We do not store payment card details; all transactions are handled by platform-specific providers.

6. Third-Party Services

We partner with the following services to provide functionality:

  • Clerk: Handles authentication and user identity.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription entitlements and purchase history using a unique user identifier.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Process all financial transactions directly.

7. Subscription Terms

SMS Manager offers Monthly and Annual auto-renewing subscriptions. Payments will be charged to your App Store or Google Play account. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your platform's account settings at any time.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account data (rules and contacts) for as long as your account is active. To comply with GDPR, users may request the permanent deletion of their data at any time by contacting us at info@applicalgap.com. Requests are processed within 30 days.
