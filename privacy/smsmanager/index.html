Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026 | Erhan Güngör | info@applicalgap.com

1. Data Collection and Usage

To provide synchronization and service continuity, the following data is stored in our server-side PostgreSQL database and linked to your account: Blocked sender names, contact names and phone numbers, and forwarding rules. This data is stored solely to operate the service.

SMS Content Ground Truth: SMS message body and content are NEVER transmitted to or stored on any server. All processing for filtering and forwarding occurs locally on your device.

2. Account and Authentication

We use Clerk (clerk.com) for authentication. Your sign-in credentials (email/phone) are processed by Clerk. Review their policy at clerk.com/privacy.

3. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Handles purchase management and entitlement checks.
  • Apple/Google: Handle all financial transactions directly. No payment card details are shared with the developer.
