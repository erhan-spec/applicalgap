● SMS Manager

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025  ·  Developer: Erhan Güngör  ·  info@applicalgap.com

1. Introduction

SMS Manager is a personal SMS management tool developed by Erhan Güngör. We take your privacy seriously. This policy transparently explains what data the application processes and how.

Short version: SMS Manager processes your data locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to our servers, stored in the cloud, or shared with third parties.

2. Data We Process

Processed locally on your device:

The application accesses and processes the following data locally on your device only:

  • SMS sender names and numbers (for blocking/forwarding rules)
  • User-created blocking and forwarding rules
  • Processing logs (which messages were blocked or forwarded)

This data is never transmitted to our servers, stored in the cloud, or shared with any third party.

Automatically collected data:

The application contains no analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or third-party tracking library. No device ID, location, camera, or microphone access is requested.

3. Permissions

The following device permissions are used solely to provide the app’s core functionality:

  • READ_SMS / RECEIVE_SMS
    To read incoming messages and process them according to your rules. Used only for blocking and forwarding decisions.
  • SEND_SMS
    For the SMS forwarding feature. Only used when you explicitly enable forwarding in the app settings.
  • READ_CONTACTS
    To allow you to create blocking rules based on sender names from your contacts. Contact data is never stored or transmitted.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored in your device’s secure storage area. The application uses the built-in security mechanisms provided by the Android/iOS platform to prevent unauthorised access.

Since no server-side data processing occurs, there is no risk of a server breach affecting your data.

5. Children’s Privacy

This application is not directed at persons under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Third-Party Services

The application is distributed via the Google Play Store and/or Apple App Store. These platforms have their own privacy policies which are outside the control of the app developer.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about any data processed by this application, to request correction or deletion of that data, and to withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the application.
